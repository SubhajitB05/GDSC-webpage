About Google Developer Student Club

Jalpaiguri Government Engineering College

GDSC JGEC is a non-profit developer group powered by Google to learn, share, connect and develop skills.

Google Developer Student Clubs are community groups for college and university students interested in Google developer technologies. By joining a DSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their communities. Know More
